Superstore Sales Analysis
SUBMITTED BY : WINNER CHIOMA UNUAGBON

Project Overview

This project involved analyzing the Superstore dataset to identify key business trends, sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior. The analysis was conducted using data analytics and visualization techniques to generate actionable business insights.

Objectives

* Evaluate overall sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Analyze regional sales trends.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior.
* Provide recommendations for business growth and profitability.
